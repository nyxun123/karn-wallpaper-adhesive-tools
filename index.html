Wallpaper Adhesive Mixing Ratio Calculator & Sample Test Record

Calculate water quantity from the ratio stated on a product label or technical data sheet, then record the conditions needed for a repeatable sample comparison. Calculations stay in your browser and are not sent to a server.

Important: This tool does not recommend a mixing ratio. Enter only the ratio supplied for the exact product being tested and confirm it with the current label or technical document.

Step 3

Interpret results without changing variables

  1. Confirm the exact product, batch, and current document revision before testing.
  2. Change only one test variable at a time when investigating a difference.
  3. Compare complaint material with an unopened or retained sample under matched conditions.
  4. Record observations rather than relying only on a photograph of the final mixture.
  5. Ask the supplier for clarification when the label, TDS, SDS, or COA references do not match.
